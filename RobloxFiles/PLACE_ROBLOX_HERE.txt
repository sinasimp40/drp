Denfi Roblox - Portable Launcher
========================================

PLACE YOUR ROBLOX FILES HERE

Copy ALL files from your Roblox installation:

  Where to find them:
  %LOCALAPPDATA%\Roblox\Versions\[version-hash]\

  Files needed:
  - RobloxPlayerBeta.exe
  - All .dll files
  - All other files in that folder

After copying, just run the launcher!
